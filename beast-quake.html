Beast Quake

Jan 8, 2011. Marshawn Lynch's legendary 67-yard run that shook the earth.

↓

It was the 2010 NFC Wild Card game. The Seattle Seahawks were hosting the defending champion New Orleans Saints. Late in the fourth quarter, Seattle was looking to run out the clock. Marshawn Lynch took the handoff... and the rest is history. Breaking tackle after tackle, he refused to go down, sending the crowd into such a frenzy that the vibrations registered on a nearby seismograph.
