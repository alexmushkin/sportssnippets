As we continually move into new ages in every aspect of our lives, we inevitably lose some of the past. Our memories are compressed and we have to work to unzip them. We want to remember the first time we felt the magnitude of a crowded stadium. The heartbreak shown on one team and the peak of humanity on the other. The wonder deep inside your child self as you believed, “maybe that could be me.” For those who were not there and for those who want to relive it, let us take you back.
